My Scavation to North America

Created by Vhyck Aquino

What is the American dream? When we think about America, our minds wander like skyscrapers of New York or the famous land of Lincoln, Illinois, Chicago. There are many landmarks in the USA.

Here's my story. In the sprawling city of Los Angeles, nestled amidst the palm trees and neon lights, lived Victor, an immigrant from the Philippines. His story was one of resilience, courage, and the pursuit of the American dream.

Victor had arrived in the United States with a backpack of dreams and a heart filled with hope. Leaving behind his family and a life of struggle in Manila, he had set out on a perilous journey northward, navigating treacherous paths and risking it all for the chance at a better life.
